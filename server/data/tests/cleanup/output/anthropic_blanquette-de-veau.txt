TITLE:
Blanquette de Veau

NOTES:
Recipe by Philippe Etchebest
Traditional French veal stew with a creamy sauce
---

METADATA:
NATIONALITY: French
AUTHOR: Philippe Etchebest
QUALITY_SCORE: 95

SPECIAL EQUIPMENT:
- Large pot (faitout)
- Chinois (fine-mesh strainer)
- Whisk

INGREDIENTS:
- 1 kg veal (shoulder, neck)
- 3 L water
- 2 fresh thyme branches
- 1 fresh bay leaf
- 4 carrots
- 2 onions
- 1 leek
- 3 garlic cloves
- 1 lemon
- 10 fresh chive sprigs
- 300 g Paris mushrooms
- 1 tbsp sunflower oil
- 60 g butter
- 60 g flour
- 20 cl liquid cream
- Coarse salt
- Pepper
- Espelette pepper

INSTRUCTIONS:
1. Blanch the veal in cold water in a large pot
2. Peel all vegetables
3. Cut carrots in half if large, onions in quarters, leek in half, remove garlic sprouts
4. Remove impurities from the broth
5. Add vegetables, bay leaf, and thyme
6. Add coarse salt and pepper
7. Squeeze half a lemon (watch for seeds)
8. Simmer for 1.5 hours on low heat
9. Strain broth, separating vegetables and meat
10. Make a roux by mixing flour and melted butter
11. Gradually incorporate broth into roux to create sauce, avoiding lumps
12. Reduce remaining broth in another pan
13. Slice mushrooms diagonally
14. Add reduced broth to sauce for enhanced flavor
15. Whip cream for 5 minutes
16. Add lemon juice to stabilize whipped cream
17. Season sauce with Espelette pepper, salt, and pepper
18. Incorporate whipped cream
19. Sauté mushrooms in hot oil until caramelized
20. Add meat and mushrooms to sauce
21. Cut cooked carrots into large beveled pieces
22. Chop chives and add to sauce with lemon zest
