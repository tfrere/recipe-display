TITLE:
Classic Chocolate Cake

NOTES:
A rich and moist chocolate cake that serves 8 people.

METADATA:
NATIONALITY: [Recipe nationality or cuisine type, if available]

BOOK: [Book title or source, if from a cookbook]

SPECIAL EQUIPMENT:
- Large bowl
- Cake tin
- Wire rack

INGREDIENTS:
- 200g flour
- 50g cocoa powder
- 200g sugar
- 2 eggs
- 100ml milk
- 100g butter (melted)
- 1 tsp baking powder

INSTRUCTIONS:

1. Preheat the oven to **180°C**.
2. In a large bowl, mix the dry ingredients (flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and baking powder).
3. Add the wet ingredients (eggs, milk, and melted butter) and mix well until combined.
4. Pour the batter into a greased cake tin.
5. Bake for **30min** or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean.
6. Let cool in the tin for **10min** before transferring to a wire rack.
7. Allow to cool completely before serving.
